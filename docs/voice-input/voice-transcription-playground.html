Interaction Details

  • Hold-to-Record: Press and hold the mic button (bottom-right of middle grid). Recording starts immediately, stops on release. For quick commands.
  • Tap Dictation: Single tap enters continuous recording mode. Tap again or wait 5s of silence to stop. For longer input.
  • Chip lifecycle: Each phrase arrives as a chip → starts 0.8s commit countdown (green progress bar fills) → text typed into terminal → chip fades to "committed" state.
  • Dismiss a chip: Tap the × button on any uncommitted chip to cancel it before it reaches the terminal. Chip slides left and fades out.
  • Swipe dismiss: Swipe a chip left to dismiss (same as tapping ×). Horizontal drag past threshold triggers dismissal.
  • Trigger word: If a phrase ends with "enter", "run", or "execute", that word is stripped and Return is auto-pressed after the text is typed.
  • Strip lifecycle: Strip slides up when recording starts, stays visible while chips are pending, slides away after all chips commit or are dismissed.
